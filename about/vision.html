Vision

For a Future Where Extraction Leaves the Earth Richer

DOKA Mining's vision is to become a leading responsible precious metals producer in Turkey, setting the benchmark for what environmentally conscious and community-sensitive mining looks like in the modern era.

We believe that the measure of a mining company's success is not only what it extracts from the earth, but what it contributes to the communities, ecosystems, and economy surrounding its operations. Our vision is production that generates enduring value, not enduring damage.

By 2030, we aim to have all active sites meeting or exceeding international ESG benchmarks, with zero-compromise environmental compliance and measurable positive community impact at every operation.

“

To be the company that proves precious metals and pristine environments are not a contradiction.

DOKA Mining Vision Statement
Mission

Responsible Extraction, Lasting Value

Our mission is to identify, develop, and operate precious metals assets in Turkey with the highest standards of geological rigor, environmental protection, and community benefit — delivering sustainable returns to all stakeholders while contributing to the national economy and upholding the values of our group.

This mission is pursued through disciplined exploration practices, responsible operational management, and a deep commitment to the regions and people who host our activities.

Our Values

The Principles We Will Not Compromise

These values are not aspirational statements. They are operational requirements that govern how DOKA behaves at every level.

Integrity

We operate with complete transparency to all stakeholders: investors, regulators, communities, and employees. No compromises, no shortcuts.

Environmental Stewardship

The natural environment is not a resource to be consumed but a responsibility to be protected. Every DOKA operation is designed to minimize and remediate its ecological footprint.

People First

The safety, dignity, and wellbeing of our employees and host communities are paramount. Our performance metrics include social outcomes alongside financial ones.

Responsibility

We accept full accountability for our decisions and their consequences. Every operation is backed by thorough planning, rigorous monitoring, and honest reporting.

Excellence

We hold ourselves to the highest technical, environmental, and governance standards — not because regulations require it, but because excellence is the foundation of lasting value.

National & Local Value

DOKA's work contributes to Turkey's strategic minerals independence, employs Turkish citizens, and generates economic activity in the regions where we operate.
