Social Responsibility

Mining is more than extraction. It is a relationship with the earth, the communities, and future generations.

Our Approach

Responsibility Is Not a Policy. It Is a Practice.

At DOKA Mining, social responsibility is embedded in every operational decision, not added as an afterthought. From initial site selection through exploration, operation, and eventual closure, we evaluate our social and environmental footprint at every step.

We define our responsibilities across four interconnected domains: the natural environment, the communities surrounding our sites, our employees, and the broader national interest. Each is taken seriously. None is subordinated to short-term economic considerations.

Environment

Protecting Turkey's Natural Heritage

Our environmental commitments go beyond legal compliance. We operate under the principle that the land we work on must be healthier when we leave than when we arrived.

Biodiversity Protection

Pre-operation ecological surveys identify protected species and habitats. Exclusion zones and mitigation measures are implemented before any ground disturbance.

Land Rehabilitation

A legally secured rehabilitation plan is prepared and funded before extraction commences. We restore disturbed land to equal or better ecological condition.

Water Stewardship

Closed-loop water management systems, real-time quality monitoring, and zero-discharge targets are applied at every site regardless of regulatory minimums.

Air Quality Monitoring

Particulate matter and dust suppression programs protect surrounding communities and ecosystems. Continuous air quality monitoring is standard at all DOKA sites.

Climate Responsibility

We actively measure and work to reduce the carbon intensity of our operations, in alignment with Turkey's national climate commitments and the group's sustainability targets.

Waste Management

All waste streams, including tailings and process chemicals, are managed under approved environmental plans with regular independent audits.

Community

Investing in the Communities That Host Our Operations

We do not extract value from communities. We create it with them.

Local Employment Priority

We commit to prioritizing local hiring at all site levels where skills and qualifications permit, and invest in training where they do not yet.

Local Supplier Preference

Goods and services procurement from local businesses is pursued actively, circulating economic benefit within host regions.

Transparent Community Dialogue

Regular consultation meetings, open information sessions, and accessible grievance channels ensure communities remain informed and heard.

Social Investment Programs

In development — replace with actual programs

DOKA intends to develop targeted social investment programs aligned with local community needs, including education, infrastructure, and vocational training.

Cultural Heritage Respect

All site activities include cultural heritage assessments. Identified heritage sites are protected and excluded from operational areas.

Health & Safety

Zero-harm culture, regular safety training, and occupational health programs apply to every worker on every DOKA site, regardless of employment type.

Policies

Our Governing Policies

DOKA Mining operates under a comprehensive set of policies aligned with the group's governance framework and international responsible mining standards.
