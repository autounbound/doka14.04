Who We Are

DOKA Madencilik

DOKA Madencilik (DOKA Mining) is a Turkish precious metals exploration and mining company. Founded on core principles of environmental responsibility, national economic contribution, and long-term value creation, DOKA focuses on identifying, developing, and operating precious metals deposits across Turkey's most geologically promising regions.

Our operations are concentrated across three licensed sites in the Central and Northeastern Anatolian provinces of Kırşehir, Bayburt, and Yozgat. Each site is managed under rigorous environmental management standards, with community engagement and land stewardship as non-negotiable commitments.

Turkey occupies a significant position in global precious metals geology. DOKA is positioned to responsibly unlock a portion of that potential, delivering value to investors, communities, and the national economy alike.
