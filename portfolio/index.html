Portfolio Overview

A Focused, High-Potential Asset Base

DOKA Mining's exploration portfolio covers three licensed areas in Central and Northeastern Anatolia. Each site was selected through rigorous geological evaluation for its potential to host economically significant precious metals mineralization, principally gold (Au) and silver (Ag).

Turkey lies within the Tethyan metallogenic belt, one of the world's most productive geological settings for precious and base metals. Our portfolio targets proven structural corridors within this belt, supported by historical exploration data and modern geoscientific methods.
