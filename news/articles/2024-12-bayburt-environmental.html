Environmental Baseline Study Completed for Bayburt Project Area

DOKA Mining has successfully completed a comprehensive environmental baseline assessment for the Bayburt license area, establishing the ecological and hydrological reference conditions for responsible exploration.

15 Dec 2024

DOKA Madencilik has finalised a comprehensive environmental baseline study for the Bayburt license area in Northeastern Anatolia. Conducted over a six-month field campaign, the study establishes the pre-exploration ecological, hydrological, and soil condition reference point against which all future environmental monitoring will be measured.

Results confirm that the Bayburt area supports a range of grassland and mixed woodland habitats. No critically endangered species were recorded within the licence boundary, though several areas of elevated ecological sensitivity have been formally incorporated into DOKA's site management protocols as no-go zones.

The baseline study forms a mandatory component of DOKA's environmental compliance framework and provides the foundation for the Environmental Impact Assessment process that will be initiated prior to any advanced exploration activities.
