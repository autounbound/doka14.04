DOKA Mining Signs Local Employment Commitment with Yozgat Municipality

A formal memorandum of understanding has been signed with the Yozgat Provincial Directorate, formalising DOKA's commitment to prioritise local hiring and supplier engagement.

28 Nov 2024

DOKA Madencilik has signed a Memorandum of Understanding with the Yozgat Provincial Directorate formalising the company's commitments to local employment and community economic participation as exploration activities commence in the region.

The MoU establishes a framework under which DOKA will give preference to qualified candidates from Yozgat province for all site-level operational and support roles. It further commits the company to prioritising local suppliers and service providers in its procurement processes where comparable quality and terms are available.

DOKA's management has stated that the Yozgat MoU reflects the company's broader approach to community relations across all three exploration sites. Similar engagement frameworks are being developed for the Kırşehir and Bayburt project areas.
