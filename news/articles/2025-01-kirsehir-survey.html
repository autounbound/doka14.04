DOKA Mining Advances Exploration Program at Kırşehir Site

Initial geophysical survey results confirm the presence of anomalous zones consistent with precious metals mineralization at the Kırşehir license area.

1 Jan 2025

DOKA Madencilik has completed the first phase of geophysical survey work at its Kırşehir license area. Results indicate the presence of multiple anomalous zones consistent with precious metals mineralization, principally gold and silver.

The survey work employed induced polarization (IP) and magnetic methods across a grid covering the highest-priority target areas. Key findings include two zones of elevated chargeability response coinciding with structural lineaments identified in earlier remote sensing analysis. These zones are prioritized for follow-up soil geochemistry and targeted drilling in subsequent phases.

All exploration activities at the Kırşehir site are conducted in full compliance with Turkish mining regulations and under DOKA's approved Environmental Management Plan.
