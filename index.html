About DOKA Mining

Where Geological Expertise Meets Environmental Responsibility

DOKA Madencilik operates at the intersection of technological precision and environmental stewardship. We benefit from the financial stability, governance infrastructure, and operational track record of an established Turkish industrial group, carrying core values of integrity, sustainability, and national economic contribution into the field of precious metals.

Our operations span three strategically selected sites across Kırşehir, Bayburt, and Yozgat provinces, each chosen for their geological promise and managed with the highest standards of responsible mining practice.
