Why Invest in DOKA Mining

The Investment Case

DOKA presents a rare combination of geological promise, institutional backing, and disciplined ESG practice in one of the world's most active base and precious metals jurisdictions.

01

Turkey's Underexplored Base and Precious Metals Potential

Turkey is located within the Tethyan metallogenic belt, a globally recognized geological setting that has produced some of the world's largest gold, silver, and base metal deposits. Despite this, large portions of Turkey's mineral-prospective terrain remain underexplored by modern methods, offering significant first-mover discovery potential. DOKA's licensed areas are positioned within proven prospective corridors across Anatolia, with concentration in Central Anatolia where the licence portfolio is densest.

02

Institutional Group Backing

DOKA benefits from the financial stability, governance infrastructure, and operational track record of an established Turkish industrial group. This institutional backing provides investors with confidence in operational management capacity and financial discipline that early-stage mining companies often lack.

03

A Portfolio, Not a Single Bet

Three licensed sites across two distinct geological settings — Kırşehir and Yozgat in Central Anatolia and Bayburt in the Northeast — provide natural geological diversification within a single investment. DOKA's broader licence portfolio extends across Anatolia, from the northwest to the northeast and southeast, providing multiple paths to value creation beyond the current primary sites.

04

ESG-Embedded Operations

Responsible mining is not a marketing position at DOKA; it is an operational requirement. Environmental management systems, community engagement protocols, and transparent governance frameworks are in place from the earliest stages. This approach reduces regulatory risk, builds community license to operate, and aligns with the investment criteria of an increasingly ESG-focused capital market.

05

Favorable Base and Precious Metals Market Dynamics

Gold continues to perform as a store of value and strategic reserve asset in an uncertain macroeconomic environment. The long-term fundamentals for base and precious metals remain constructive. DOKA's early-stage positioning enables investors to participate in the value creation journey from exploration through to potential production, a profile that historically offers the most significant return upside in the mining sector.
