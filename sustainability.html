Sustainability

Our systematic approach to operating in a manner the earth, communities, and future generations can benefit from.

Our Commitment

Sustainability Is the Foundation, Not the Finish

Sustainability at DOKA is not a department or a report. It is the organizing logic of every operational decision, from exploration licensing through site rehabilitation. We pursue it because it is the right way to operate, and because it is the only way to generate the long-term value our stakeholders deserve.

Our sustainability framework is built on three pillars: environmental integrity, social responsibility, and governance transparency. Each pillar has defined targets, measurable indicators, and independent verification.

Environmental Pillar I

Zero net biodiversity loss at all sites by 2027

Social Pillar II

Minimum 70% local workforce target at each operation

Governance Pillar III

Annual independent ESG audit and public disclosure

* Target figures are indicative placeholders pending formal ESG framework publication.

Environmental Management

How We Protect What We Work Within

Our Environmental Management System (EMS) governs all stages of activity at DOKA sites. It is structured to exceed Turkish regulatory requirements and align with internationally recognized responsible mining frameworks.

Environmental baseline studies completed before any site activity commences

Continuous water, air, and soil quality monitoring throughout operations

Mandatory post-closure rehabilitation bonding for all licensed areas

Independent environmental auditing on an annual basis

Incident reporting and corrective action frameworks with zero-tolerance approach
